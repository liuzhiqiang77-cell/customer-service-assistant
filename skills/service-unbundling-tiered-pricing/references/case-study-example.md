# Service Unbundling Case Study: SAS Airline

## Original Situation
- Free coffee and bun service costing $400,000/year
- Low quality generating customer complaints
- Cost center with no revenue

## Implementation
1. **Eliminated free service**: Removed complimentary coffee and bun
2. **Introduced premium**: Full breakfast for $2 (half the cost of train breakfast)
3. **Generated profit**: 50 cents per serving
4. **Gathered feedback**: Customers said "I'd be glad to pay a dollar for coffee and a bun"
5. **Reintroduced basic**: Coffee and bun for $1

## Results
- Transformed $400,000 cost into revenue stream
- Improved customer satisfaction
- Price aligned with perceived value
- Multiple price points captured different customer segments
