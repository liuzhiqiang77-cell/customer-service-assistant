# Detailed Role Transformations

## Top Executive: New Responsibilities

### Visionary
- Define and communicate clear business strategy
- Paint picture of what the company can become
- Keep the "big picture" in mind

### Informer
- Share information openly throughout organization
- Ensure frontline has context to make decisions
- Remove information bottlenecks

### Teacher
- Train employees to take responsibility for reaching goals
- Educate on business context and market needs
- Develop leadership capabilities in others

### Inspirer
- Create environment for confidence
- Motivate through shared vision, not fear
- Emotionally expressive and engaging

## Middle Management: New Responsibilities

### Problem Analyzer
- Identify root causes of operational issues
- Develop solutions for systemic problems
- Prevent recurrence of customer service failures

### Resource Manager
- Ensure frontline teams have what they need
- Allocate budget, tools, and support effectively
- Remove obstacles to frontline success

### Frontline Supporter
- Serve the frontline, not control them
- Facilitate rather than direct
- Enable frontline to serve customers

## Frontline Employee: New Authority

### Decision Authority
- Make individual decisions without permission
- Solve customer problems immediately
- Take initiative in ambiguous situations

### Example Actions
- Retrieving a forgotten ticket via limo service
- Offering compensation for service failures
- Adapting procedures to fit customer needs
- Seizing opportunities to create memorable interactions

### Prerequisites for Success
- Receive accurate, up-to-date information
- Training in speed and courtesy
- Clear understanding of business goals
- Support from middle management
